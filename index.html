Welcome to Stitch Craft

Antony (Tony) D’Silva co-founder of StitchCraft LLC came to Michigan from Kerala, India in 1987 at the age of 22. He has worked in the clothing industry as a manufacture and a tailor for 44 years. Tony started his career as a tailor at the age of 14 in his home country. He has worked in the film industry as a tailor and was sponsored to the USA from a talent show for a better job opportunity. After arriving here in the USA he began working with a custom tailoring business. Tony and his previous partner Ernest Pressley owned and operated Exclusive Men’s Wear from 2001 to 2015. He has now started another manufacturing business StitchCraft LLC custom clothing in 2018. This has also guided he and his partner in the sports attire industry. They are growing both the custom clothing and the sports divisions together.

Isaac “Nate” Jefferson I, Has worked in a variety of industries In corporate America and as a entrepreneur. Isaac has a passion for serving people with customer service in every way. Isaac has worked in sales for the pass 23 years. In 2018 the entrepreneur bug bit him again and he and his partner Anthony D’Silva started Stitchcraft LLC.
